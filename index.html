TradingBot 3000

TradingBot 3000 is an experimental workspace for testing automated strategies with paper trades, visible risk controls, and explanations for why a strategy wants to act.

Planned product pillars

  • Paper-trading simulation before any real-world integration.
  • Strategy sandbox with explicit assumptions and limits.
  • Readable risk controls and emergency shutdown states.
  • Decision explanations instead of mysterious green arrows.

Current status

Interface and product architecture are in progress. No release date, price, performance benchmark, or financial outcome is being claimed.
